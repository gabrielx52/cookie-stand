We bring the Puget Sound's most iconic flavors together: gluten-free baked goods and commercial fishing by-product.

about us

A Seattle institution with locations scatterd throughout the city. We handcraft our small-batch, artisinal products in a derelict fishing vessel ran aground in the Puget Sound. All of our ingredients are sustainably harvested from localy owned Whole Foods dumpsters by our hard working staff of urban foragers. We hope you enjoy our cookies as much as we enjoy crafting them for you.
